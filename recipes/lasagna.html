Vegan Lasagna Recipe

This is what your amazing vegan lasagna will look like!


Ingredients

  • 180 g dried red lentils
  • (2) 25-ounce jars marinara sauce
  • 129 g raw cashews
  • 454 g firm tofu patted dry
  • 22 g nutritional yeast
  • 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice from about 2 lemons
  • 60 g baby spinach
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 box lasagna noodles (about 12 noodles) regular, gluten free or whole grain
  • vegan cheese 200g

Steps

  1. Cook the red lentils.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
  3. Make the Cashew-Tofu Ricotta
  4. Assembling the lasagna
  5. While the lasagna is cooking, make your Vegan Mozzarella Cheese
  6. After 40 minutes, remove the foil and spoon on the mozzarella (or sprinkle the cheese all over). Place the lasagna back in the oven and bake for 20 more minutes, uncovered. Remove from oven, let cool for at least 15 minutes and serve.
